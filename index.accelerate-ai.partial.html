Accelerate AI adoption in your company

Generative AI unlocks new frontiers for enterprises and scale-ups. The GenAI Proof of Concept service by Neurons Lab and AWS is designed to identify and evaluate generative AI use cases and build prototypes.

Participants will leave with a clear roadmap for selecting and tuning LLMs, determining whether to train new models or use pre-existing ones, and a list of hosting options, such as Amazon SageMaker and Amazon Bedrock. We will build a working PoC to validate assumptions and reduce risk in future investments in GenAI technology.
