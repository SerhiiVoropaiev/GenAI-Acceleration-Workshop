Turn your ideas into detailed strategy and tactics for deploying GenAI, 75-100% funded by AWS

Generative AI unlocks new frontiers for enterprises and scale-ups. The GenAI workshop by Neurons Lab and AWS is designed to identify and evaluate generative AI use cases, as well as build prototypes. Participants will leave with a clear roadmap for selecting and tuning LLMs, determining whether to train new models or use pre-existing ones, and a list of hosting options, such as Amazon SageMaker and Amazon Bedrock. We will build a working PoC to validate assumptions and reduce risk in future investments in GenAI technology.
