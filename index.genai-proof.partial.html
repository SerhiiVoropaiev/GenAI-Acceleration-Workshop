How the GenAI Workshop Works

GenAI Workshop + PoC is a program that lasts between 1 to 2 weeks. It concludes with practical recommendations, a Proof of Concept, and a strategy for further implementation of GenAI.

(1 week)

Phase I: Discovery

  • Prioritize the top GenAI use cases for your business
  • Explore monetization and estimate the solution's ROI
  • Evaluate potential foundation models

(1 week)

Phase II: Proof of Concept

  • Design and deploy GenAI Proof of Concept for the top use case
  • Test different LLMs with your data
  • Select and fine-tune foundation model

Full-scale GenAI Integration

Further steps

  • Extend partnership for further GenAI value-capturing
  • Utilize GenAI Solution AWS funding for further implementation
  • Scale cloud and MLOps infrastructure
