How the GenAI Proof of Concept Works

GenAI Proof of Concept is a program that lasts between 4 to 8 weeks. It concludes with practical recommendations, a Proof of Concept, and a strategy for further implementation of GenAI.

(1-2 Weeks)

Phase I: Discovery

  • Identify and prioritize the top GenAI use cases for your business
  • Explore monetization and estimate the solution's ROI
  • Evaluation of potential foundation models
  • Tailored recommendations and implementation documentation

(3-7 Weeks)

Phase II: Proof of Concept

  • Iterative testing of LLMs with your data
  • Foundation model selection and tuning guidance
  • GenAI Proof of Concept design and deployment
  • Recommended GenerativeAI strategy roadmap

Full-scale GenAI Integration

Further steps

  • Extended partnership for further GenAI value-capturing
  • Full-cycle deployment with observability and security
  • Jumpstart AWS funding for further GenAI implementation
  • Utilization of proprietary data and enterprise-wide feedback loops
